Stay Healthy

Eating nutritious foods, staying active, and getting enough sleep are key to keeping your body strong and your mind sharp.

The Importance of Exercise for Kids

Regular physical activity is essential for children's growth and development. It helps build strong muscles and bones, improves cardiovascular health, and boosts self-esteem.

Exercise also helps kids manage stress and maintain a healthy weight.

Quick Health Tips

  • Eat a rainbow of fruits and veggies every day.
  • Drink plenty of water to stay hydrated.
  • Get at least 60 minutes of exercise daily.
  • Wash your hands regularly to prevent germs.
  • Get enough sleep for a happy, healthy body.
