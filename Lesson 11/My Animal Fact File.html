Snow Leopard

Type: Big Cat

Lives in: Mountain ranges of Central Asia

Diet: Deer, goats, and wild sheep

Cool Fact: Can leap up to 15 metres in one jump!

Emperor Penguin

Type: Flightless Bird

Lives in: Antarctica

Diet: Fish, krill, and squid

Cool Fact: Can dive deeper than 500 metres down into the ocean!

Peregrine Falcon

Type: Bird of Prey

Lives in: Every continent except Antarctica

Diet: Other birds and bats

Cool Fact: The fastest animal on Earth, reaching speeds over 320 km/h when diving!

Axolotl

Type: Amphibian

Lives in: Lake Xochimilco near Mexico City

Diet: Worms, insects, and small fish

Cool Fact: Can perfectly regenerate its limbs, tail, and even parts of its brain!
