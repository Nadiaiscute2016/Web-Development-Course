☀️ My Morning Routine

Every morning I wake up at 6:00 AM. I brush my teeth, take my bath, wear my school uniform, and eat a healthy breakfast. After breakfast, I pray, pack my school bag, and check my timetable before leaving for school. I like arriving early because it helps me prepare for the day.

🧠 Subject Descriptions

➕ Mathematics

Mathematics teaches students how to solve problems using numbers, shapes, measurements, and calculations. It is useful in everyday life.

🔬 Science

Science helps students understand nature, animals, plants, electricity, the human body, and space.

📖 English Language

English teaches reading, writing, speaking, spelling, and grammar. It helps students communicate clearly.

💻 Computer Studies

Computer Studies teaches students how to use computers, type documents, create projects, and learn coding.

🌍 Social Studies

Social Studies teaches students about people, communities, cultures, and different countries around the world.
