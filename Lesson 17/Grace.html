Grace's Story 🌸

Grace was a sweet and creative 6-year-old girl who loved dolls, singing, and baking cookies. She had a caring heart and always enjoyed making things that could bring happiness to others.

Grace came to International Hope Adoption Center after her family went through a difficult situation and she needed a safe place where she could be cared for, supported, and encouraged.

When Grace arrived at the center, she brought her favorite doll with her. She loved pretending that her doll was a customer at her very own bakery, where she would create imaginary cookies and cakes.

The staff soon discovered Grace's love for baking and music. During activities at the center, she would sing while decorating cookies and share her creations with the other children.

Grace dreams of becoming a baker and songwriter so she can combine her two favorite things: creating delicious treats and making music that makes people smile.

Grace hopes to find a forever family who will bake with her, listen to her songs, and support her colorful dreams. 🧁🎶🌸
