Mia's Story 💛

Mia was a gentle and imaginative 7-year-old girl who loved music, puzzles, and story time. She enjoyed discovering new songs and creating her own little adventures through stories.

Mia came to International Hope Adoption Center after changes in her family life meant she needed a safe and caring place where she could grow. The center welcomed her and helped her begin a new chapter.

When Mia first arrived, she was a little nervous about being somewhere new. She found comfort in the activity room, where she discovered books, puzzles, and musical instruments that helped her feel at home.

One afternoon, Mia created a small song about friendship and performed it for the other children. Everyone loved her sweet voice and the happiness she shared. The staff encouraged her love of music and creativity.

Mia dreams of becoming a singer and sharing her music with people everywhere. She hopes to find a forever family who will listen to her songs, support her dreams, and celebrate her talents. 🎤💛
