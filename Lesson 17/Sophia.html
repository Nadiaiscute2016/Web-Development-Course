Sophia's Story 💜

Sophia was a cheerful and creative 7-year-old girl who loved music, dancing, and making up performances for her stuffed animals. She had a big imagination and always dreamed of being on a stage someday.

Sophia came to International Hope Adoption Center after she was unable to receive the care and support she needed at home. The center became a safe place where she could feel loved, protected, and encouraged to keep following her dreams.

At the adoption center, Sophia shared her love of dancing and singing with everyone around her. The staff admired her confidence, kindness, and the way she could bring happiness to others.

Sophia hopes to find a forever family who will dance with her, support her talents, and help her chase her dream of becoming a famous dancer. 🎵💜
