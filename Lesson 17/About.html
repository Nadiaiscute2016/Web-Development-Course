About International Hope Child Adoption Center

Founded in 2018, International Hope Child Adoption Center was created with one simple mission: to ensure that every child has the opportunity to grow up in a safe, loving, and supportive family. Over the years, our center has cared for children from different backgrounds, providing them with education, healthcare, emotional support, and a place to call home while they wait for their forever families.

🌸 Our Mission

We believe that every child deserves love, security, and the chance to achieve their dreams. Our dedicated staff works closely with children and adoptive families to make the adoption journey safe, smooth, and rewarding for everyone involved.

📋 Adoption Requirements

  • Applicants must be at least 25 years old.
  • Must provide proof of a stable income.
  • Must have a safe and suitable home environment.
  • Must pass a background and child safety check.
  • Must complete an adoption interview and home visit.
  • Must demonstrate the ability to provide long-term care, love, and support.

👨‍👩‍👧 Adoption Process

  1. Browse our children's profiles and learn about each child.
  2. Submit an adoption application.
  3. Attend an interview with our adoption specialists.
  4. Complete a home study and background check.
  5. Meet and spend time with the child through supervised visits.
  6. Receive final approval and complete the legal adoption process.
  7. Welcome your child into their forever home and receive post-adoption support.

❤️ Why Choose Us?

International Hope Child Adoption Center is committed to treating every child with dignity, compassion, and respect. Our experienced team works tirelessly to match children with loving families, ensuring that each adoption is based on the best interests of the child. Together, we can help build brighter futures—one family at a time.
