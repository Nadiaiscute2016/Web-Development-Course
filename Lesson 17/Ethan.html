Ethan's Story 🦖

Ethan was a bright and curious 10-year-old boy who loved learning about the world around him. From a young age, he was fascinated by dinosaurs and spent hours creating dinosaur worlds with his building blocks.

After a difficult family situation left Ethan without someone who could care for him, he was brought to International Hope Adoption Center, where he was welcomed by a team that wanted to give him a safe and loving place to grow.

At the center, Ethan quickly became known for his creativity, kindness, and endless questions about fossils and ancient creatures. The staff encouraged his love of science and helped him continue chasing his dream of becoming a paleontologist.

Ethan hopes that one day he will find a forever family who will support his dreams, build new memories with him, and join him on exciting adventures.
