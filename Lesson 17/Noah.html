Noah's Story 💚

Noah was a curious and creative 10-year-old boy who loved discovering new things. His favorite activities were building amazing creations with blocks and learning about dinosaurs that lived millions of years ago.

Noah came to International Hope Adoption Center after his family situation changed and he needed a safe and caring place to stay. The center welcomed him and gave him a supportive environment where he could continue growing and exploring his interests.

At the adoption center, Noah became known for his imagination and his love of science. He enjoyed creating dinosaur worlds with his building blocks and sharing interesting facts about prehistoric creatures with others.

Noah dreams of becoming a paleontologist one day so he can discover fossils, study dinosaurs, and uncover secrets from the past. He hopes to find a forever family who will encourage his curiosity and join him on his adventures. 🦖💚
