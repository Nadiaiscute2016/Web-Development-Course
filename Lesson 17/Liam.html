Liam's Story 💙

Liam was an energetic and determined 8-year-old boy who loved anything with wheels. From toy cars racing across the floor to solving tricky puzzles, he was always curious about how things worked.

After a change in his family circumstances meant he needed a new place to grow and be cared for, Liam came to International Hope Adoption Center. The center welcomed him with open arms and gave him a safe environment where he could feel supported.

Liam quickly made friends at the center through his love of football and games. The staff encouraged his creativity and his big dream of becoming a race car driver one day.

Liam hopes to find a forever family who will cheer him on, play football with him, and support him as he follows his dreams on and off the track. 🏎️💙
