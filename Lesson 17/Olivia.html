Olivia's Story 💜

Olivia was a sweet and creative 8-year-old girl who loved painting, reading, and learning about butterflies. She often used art to express her feelings and create beautiful worlds filled with color.

Olivia's early years were difficult. Her parents left her and she was unable to receive the care and support every child deserves. A caring neighbor noticed that Olivia needed help and contacted child services so she could be protected and taken somewhere safe.

After child services learned about Olivia's situation, she was brought to International Hope Adoption Center. The staff welcomed her with kindness and helped her feel safe while she adjusted to her new surroundings.

At the center, Olivia found comfort in her favorite activities. She filled her days painting pictures, reading books, and drawing butterflies. The staff loved seeing her creativity grow and encouraged her artistic talents.

Olivia dreams of becoming an artist one day and hopes to find a forever family who will support her imagination, celebrate her artwork, and help her create a bright future. 🎨💜
