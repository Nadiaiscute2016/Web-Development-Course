Lucas's Story 💚

Lucas was a curious and adventurous 10-year-old boy who loved animals, toy trucks, and cartoons. He was always asking questions about nature and dreaming about exploring the world around him.

Lucas came to International Hope Adoption Center after his family situation changed and he needed a safe, stable place where he could receive care and support. The center welcomed him and helped him feel comfortable.

When Lucas arrived, he brought a small toy truck that he loved playing with. He imagined it was an explorer vehicle traveling through forests, mountains, and places filled with amazing animals.

At the center, Lucas discovered the garden and became fascinated by the birds, insects, and plants around him. He started drawing animals and keeping notes about the creatures he learned about.

The staff noticed Lucas's curiosity and his love for protecting animals. He dreams of becoming a wildlife explorer so he can discover new places and help care for nature.

Lucas hopes to find a forever family who will join him on adventures, encourage his curiosity, and support his dream of exploring the wild. 🐾💚
