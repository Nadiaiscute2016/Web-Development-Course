Ava's Story 🧡

Ava was a bright and cheerful 6-year-old girl who loved coloring, dancing, and playing outside. She had a wonderful imagination and could turn almost anything into a fun game.

Ava's life changed when her family went through a difficult situation and they were no longer able to provide the care she needed. A family friend noticed that Ava needed help and contacted child services so she could find a safe and supportive place.

Ava arrived at International Hope Adoption Center with a small bag of her favorite coloring supplies. At first, she was shy, but soon she began drawing colorful pictures and performing little dances for the other children.

The staff discovered that Ava had a special talent for making people smile. She loved creating happy moments and bringing energy into the center.

Ava dreams of becoming a children's entertainer so she can spread joy through dancing, laughter, and creativity. She hopes to find a forever family who will support her dreams and celebrate who she is. 🌈🧡
