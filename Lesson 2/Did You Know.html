Fact 1: The Octopus

Did you know that octopuses have three hearts? Two pump blood to the gills, while the third pumps it to the rest of the body. When an octopus swims, the heart that delivers blood to the body actually stops beating!

Octopus Intelligence

Octopuses are incredibly intelligent creatures. They have been observed using tools, solving puzzles, and even escaping from enclosures. Their ability to learn and adapt is truly remarkable!

Fact 2: Lightning

Did you know that lightning is hotter than the surface of the sun?

A lightning bolt can reach temperatures of up to 30,000 Kelvin (53,540 degrees Fahrenheit), while the surface of the sun is around 5,500 Kelvin (9,932 degrees Fahrenheit). That's why lightning can cause such intense damage!
